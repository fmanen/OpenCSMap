    About

    OpenCSMap is a system for geolocate Computer Science papers. Our main goal is to generate geograhical information about the papers and show how Computer Science research is advancing all over the world. How do we do this? Our work is based on DBLP database. We used this data to find authors' affiliations, and then linked a paper with an affiliation, based on their authors information. For simplicity, we linked a paper with only one affiliation for now. We are looking forward to improve this in the future. :)

    How does a search work?

    Our search is a text search. The system looks the terms of interest on title of the papers, title of journals and title of conferences.

    Why my paper isn't on the map?

    That's because we couldn't find an affiliation for any of the authors of the paper.

    Why some authors have a '000X' number next to their names?

    Because DBLP uses a number to diferentiate authors with the exact same name.
